Axis

Communities are organized by computed reading level on the x axis.
Communities are organized by computed activity level on the y axis.
Community size is denoted by bubble radius.

Zoom & Pan

The data can be zoomed and panned with the scroll wheel and the mouse. Scroll in/out to zoom, click and drag to pan through the data. The reset button returns all nodes back to default size, zoom, and position.

Slider

Use the slider on the left to manipulate the minimum and maximum radius of the bubbles. Sliding the lower handle affects the minimum, sliding the upper affects the maximum. Handles can be moved back to their default positions with the reset button.

Dropdown

Choose from any of the options in the drop down menu on the right to see more in-depth information.
